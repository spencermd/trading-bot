# 📋 SEC FORM 4 - Insider Trading Guide

---

## WHAT IS SEC FORM 4?

**Required filing** when company insiders (execs, directors, 10% owners) trade company stock.

---

## KEY METRICS

### Insider Buy/Sell Ratio
- **High ratio (>1)** = Insiders buying more than selling = BULLISH
- **Low ratio (<1)** = Insiders selling = BEARISH

### Historical Performance
- Insiders historically buy at lows, sell at highs
- Track record: When insiders buy, stock often outperforms

---

## FILING TYPES

| Form | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Form 4** | Insider trade (buy/sell) |
| **13D** | Activist investor (5%+ owner) - Changes coming |
| **13G** | Passive investor (5%+ owner) |
| **13F** | Institutional quarterly holdings |

## TRADING STRATEGY

### When Insiders Buy:
1. Filter: Form 4, Purchase only
2. Check: CEO/CFO buying
3. Verify: Recent (last 7 days)
4. Research: Company fundamentals
5. Enter: If aligns with other signals

### Key Signal:
- **Multiple insiders buying** = Strong signal
- **CEO buying** = Very strong
- **Large purchase ($100K+)** = Serious play
