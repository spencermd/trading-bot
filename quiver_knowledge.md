## KEY DATASETS

### 1. Congress Trading 🏛️
**What:** Track what politicians are buying/selling
**Why:** They have access to non-public info
**Track:** Nancy Pelosi, MTG, JD Vance, etc.

### 2. Insider Trading 💼
**What:** SEC Form 4 filings (like openinsider)
**Why:** Insiders know company details

### 3. Institutional Holdings 🏦
**What:** What hedge funds are buying
**Why:** Smart money moves markets

### 4. Government Contracts 💰
**What:** Which companies get government contracts
**Why:** Guaranteed revenue = stable

### 5. Corporate Lobbying 📣
**What:** Companies spending on lobbying
**Why:** Access to policy makers

### 6. Google Trends 🔍
**What:** Search volume for stocks
**Why:** Retail interest = momentum

### 7. Social Media 📱
**What:** Social sentiment
**Why:** Viral potential

### 8. App Store Ratings ⭐
**What:** App sentiment for tech companies
**Why:** Product health indicator
