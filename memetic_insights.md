## 🎯 Actionable Strategies

### 1. VIRAL COEFFICIENT STRATEGY
**Buy coins with high social score + small market cap**
- High "meme potential" = low mcap + high buzz
- Trigger: Coin trending + <$50M cap = potential 10x
- Example: SKATE, POPCAT patterns

### 2. ATTENTION MOMENTUM STRATEGY  
**Most talked about = biggest moves**
- Track social mentions vs price
- Gap = opportunity (high attention, low price)
- Trigger: High volume + social spike

### 3. COMMUNITY PUMP PATTERN
**Watch for cooperative emergence**
- Doge → Pepe → PEPE pattern
- Trigger: Reddit/Twitter community building
- Signal: Unusual volume in small caps

### 4. FOMO DETECTOR (Memetic Hazard)
**Recognize when FOMO is viral = danger**
- When everyone talks about "to the moon" = top likely
- Inverse indicator: Extreme greed = sell signal
